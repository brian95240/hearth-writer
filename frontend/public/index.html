THE 7 ARCHETYPES

Hearth Writer adapts to your creative workflow

1. COMIC ARCHITECT

Visual continuity across issues. Visual Tag Injection tracks costume states, damage, and transformations.

2. GLOBAL NOVELIST

Deep narrative callback across 1000+ pages. Shadow Nodes track "Open Loops" and foreshadowing.

3. CHILDREN'S AUTHOR

Simple vocabulary for ages 6-8. Lexile Grammar automatically restricts complexity.

4. SCREENWRITER

Rapid formatting & flow. Auto-detects INT./EXT. scene headers and formats accordingly.

5. TV SHOWRUNNER

Arc dependencies across seasons. Logic Locks prevent killing characters needed later.

6. PLAYWRIGHT

Lyrics vs. Dialogue vs. Blocking. Stage Mode splits UI into dedicated columns.

7. GAME DESIGNER

Branching logic (IF/ELSE). Logic Nodes batch-generate all possible outcomes.
