About Hearth Writer

Hearth Writer is a revolutionary authorship engine designed for writers who value privacy, performance, and creative freedom. Built on a Collapse-to-Zero architecture, it provides professional-grade writing tools while maintaining minimal resource usage.

Whether you're crafting a novel, screenplay, comic book, or any other creative work, Hearth Writer adapts to your specific needs with specialized archetypes and advanced features like Shadow Nodes for narrative continuity and local AI integration for enhanced creativity.

With tiered licensing options from free to enterprise, Hearth Writer grows with your creative journey, offering the tools you need at every stage of your writing career.
