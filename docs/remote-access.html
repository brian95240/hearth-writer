Access Hearth Writer from anywhere - mobile, tablet, or another computer

üè† Same Network (WiFi) Access

If your mobile device is on the same WiFi network as your computer running Hearth Writer, setup is simple:

Step 1: Find Your Computer's IP Address

In your Ubuntu terminal, run:

hostname -I

This will show something like: 192.168.1.105

Step 2: Access from Mobile

On your phone or tablet browser, go to:

http://YOUR_IP_ADDRESS:8000

Example: http://192.168.1.105:8000

Note: The Hearth Writer server must be running on your computer (bash start.sh) for this to work.

üìã Tailscale Setup Instructions

Setup on Windows (Your Hearth Writer PC)

  1. Go to tailscale.com/download/windows
  2. Download and install Tailscale
  3. Click the Tailscale icon in your system tray
  4. Sign in with Google, Microsoft, or GitHub
  5. Note your Tailscale IP (looks like 100.x.x.x)
Your Tailscale IP will be shown in the app. It stays the same even when your regular IP changes.

Setup on Mac (Your Hearth Writer Mac)

  1. Go to tailscale.com/download/mac
  2. Download from Mac App Store or direct download
  3. Open Tailscale from Applications
  4. Click "Connect" and sign in
  5. Note your Tailscale IP (looks like 100.x.x.x)

Setup on Linux (Your Hearth Writer PC)

curl -fsSL https://tailscale.com/install.sh | sh

Then start Tailscale:

sudo tailscale up

Get your Tailscale IP:

tailscale ip -4

Setup on Mobile (iPhone/Android)

  1. iPhone: Download Tailscale from App Store
  2. Android: Download Tailscale from Play Store
  3. Open the app and sign in with the same account as your PC
  4. Toggle the connection ON
  5. Open your browser and go to: http://100.x.x.x:8000 (your PC's Tailscale IP)

‚ùì FAQ

Q: Does my computer need to stay on?

Yes, Hearth Writer runs locally on your computer. For remote access, your computer must be powered on with the server running.

Q: Can I access from multiple devices at once?

Yes! Multiple devices can connect to the same Hearth Writer server simultaneously.

Q: Is my writing data secure over remote access?

With Tailscale, your connection is encrypted end-to-end. Your data never leaves your devices - it goes directly from your phone to your computer through an encrypted tunnel.

Q: What if my IP address changes?

Tailscale gives you a stable IP (100.x.x.x) that never changes, even if your home internet IP changes. This is one of its biggest advantages.
